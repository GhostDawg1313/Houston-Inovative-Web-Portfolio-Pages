Extended Bio

More About My Journey

Beyond technology, my story includes music, craftsmanship, family, and community. This page captures highlights adapted from my personal Google Sites About page.

Musician, Producer, Songwriter

I am the son of internationally known jazz drummer Curly Martin and the brother of Grammy Award winner Terrace Martin. I am a producer, songwriter, and musician who plays multiple instruments. I started young and joined a band through the North Omaha Boys Club.

Today I am back in a band called Rhythm City, planning a reunion and continuing to share music that brings people together.

Creative Projects

During the pandemic, I discovered a passion for pallet woodworking. With online learning and consistency, I built pieces for our backyard and developed a practical design mindset.

I also enjoy creating spaces for outside lounging and meaningful conversation, along with exploring great food and local bar-and-grill spots.

Family and Lifestyle

My world includes my fiancee Rita and our dog Buddy. Together we prioritize family, travel, and quality time around our firepit, garden, and home.

We aim to visit as many states as possible and keep building memories through shared experiences.
